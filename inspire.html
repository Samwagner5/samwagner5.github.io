Johnathan Sims

Jonathan Sims is a writer, performer, and games designer whose work primarily focuses on the macabre, the grotesque, and the gentle touch of creeping dread. He is the mind and the voice behind acclaimed horror podcast The Magnus Archives.

Johnathan Sims helped build Rust Quil, which is now a large worldwide independent leading production company. Alexander Newell took a chance on a guy he casually knew. Now The Magnus Archives has a cult-like following that backed a spin-off called The Magnus Protocol. Johnathan Sims puts everything into his podcasts and books. It is very interesting that he is this passionate about telling scary stories!
