Jonathan Sims is a writer, performer, and games designer whose work primarily focuses on the macabre, the grotesque, and the gentle touch of creeping dread. He is the mind and the voice behind acclaimed horror podcast The Magnus Archives.

Johnathan Sims helped build Rusty Quil, which is now a worldwide independent leading production company. Alexander Newell (the founder of Rusty Quil) took a chance on a guy he casually knew. Now The Magnus Archives has a cult-like following that backed a spin-off called The Magnus Protocol. Johnathan Sims puts everything into his podcasts and books. It is very inspiring that he is this passionate about telling scary stories!
